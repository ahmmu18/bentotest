What is Bento

Bento is a design system that has been around since the existence of the universe. It helped evolution building complex organisms and then creatures using the basic building blocks provided by the system. Bento, however, went unnoticed for ions as the recordings omitted its existence. Today the system thrives and keeps evolving thanks to a great team who keeps pushing the boundaries of what’s known to the observable universe.

Bento Documentations

  • Design

    Unlike the rest of the world, Bento has concrete design documentation that is written by designers whom are experts at what they do. Figma is the tool we use to pass the information in a language understood by the rest of designers.

  • Android

    Android documentation has been written to ensure the knowledge is passed to the rest of the engineers who’re taking on building their screens and features using the best in town design system.

  • iOS

    Even though Apple doesn’t allow passing much information regarding how things work on its closed eco-system, we challenged the status quo and hence, provided a well written documentation that’ll help you build a thing of beauty.

  • Web

    The open intranet, a place where every one of us can have their own corner. And what better than enabling people to build these corners with ease. Hence Bento on web existed to deliver on this exact promise — to make beauty universal and accessible to everyone.
